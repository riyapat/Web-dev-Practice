HELPING HAND FOUNDATION

--- what we do ---

The Helping Hands Foundation is a non-profit organization, which works with public inconvenience in their normal
life.We work on two projects – the Accommodation Project and the Survivor Project. The Accommodation
Project provides free accommodation to the child and the family during treatment in Dharamshalas surrounding
the hospital and the Survivor Project works towards regular testing and rehabilitation of
children who beat cancer.
The Helping Hands Foundation is a fund raising entity and works in conjunction with Tata Memorial Hospital’s ImPaCCT Foundation.
The Helping Hands Foundation is supported and endorsed by many celebrities and individuals. We hope we can inspire you to do your part as well.
